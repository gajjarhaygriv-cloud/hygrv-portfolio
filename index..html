About Me

The Mind
Behind It

I’m a product design major with a focus on building thoughtful, functional solutions, complemented by a minor in interaction design that shapes how users engage with them.

My work explores the balance between physical form and digital behavior — from concept development to refined user experiences. I focus on creating products that are not only well-designed, but intuitive, responsive, and meaningful in use.

“I don’t just design screens — I design products, systems, and the flows that connect form with function. From physical builds to digital interactions, I focus on the small details that make an experience feel intuitive, tangible, and alive.”
